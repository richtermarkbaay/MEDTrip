HCor - Hospital do Coração (Hospital for the Heart) is a specialized hospital providing treatment of cardiovascular diseases, including 37 other medical specialities: orthopedics, traumatology, gastroenterology, oncology, neurology, sports medicine, nutriology, diagnostic imaging and more.

HCor occupies three adjoined buildings with 216 beds: 40 are intensive care for children and adults, 13 for the Coronary Department and 8 for emergencies. HCor has Joint International (JCI) accreditation, a certificate of accreditation with total conformity of 98.33% out of the 1,138 measurement elements, meeting quality standards focusing on patient and management.
